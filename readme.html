READ ME

Explination

PART 1: "dont judge a book by its cover"

The entire page appears to be black.

A quick glance and one might find it pointless, maybe even an error.

It is not unitl one interacts with the page that any nuance is noticeable.

PART 2: From dark to light.

Considering this was created in the peak of winter 2020,

this work mimics the transition from the dark and dull mood of the winter, to the bright mood

of spring & summer, as well as hope for the post-Covid world.

PART 3: Regression to the mean.

Finally, this work shows that the transition to light is not final.

Fall/Winter always comes after spring/summer, spring/summer always follows Fall/Winter, and Covid won't disappear forever.
